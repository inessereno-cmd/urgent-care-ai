How It Works

From insurance card to patient responsibility in four steps

Clearpay runs a complete financial clearance workflow automatically — no manual lookups, no spreadsheets, no guessing.

Step 01
🪪

Eligibility Inquiry

Clearpay queries the payer in real time using member ID, group number, payer ID, and date of birth — returning a structured eligibility response in under 5 seconds.

Step 02
🔍

Benefits Extraction

The engine isolates every benefit relevant to an urgent care visit: deductible, deductible met YTD, copay, coinsurance, out-of-pocket max, and OOP met to date.

Step 03
⚙️

Benefits Processing

Benefits are applied against your clinic's fee schedule and collection policy — factoring in contracted rates, payer-specific rules, and visit type.

Step 04
✅

Patient Responsibility Output

A clean, itemized figure: amount due now, amount to bill post-adjudication, and a confidence level — with plain-language explanation for staff and patient.

Urgent Care Native

Understands UC-specific CPT codes, urgent-care benefit tiers distinct from primary care and ED, and time-of-service collection expectations.

📐

Policy Enforcement

Clearpay enforces your collection policy — collect copay only, collect estimated coinsurance, or collect full estimated responsibility. Your rules, automatically applied.

Payer Fluency

Speaks 270/271 EDI, payer portal APIs, and direct connections — translating complex eligibility data into plain language your front desk can act on immediately.

Full Auditability

Every estimate shows exactly which benefits were used, what the fee schedule line items are, and how the math was done. No black boxes.

Adjudication Feedback Loop

When claims adjudicate, Clearpay captures variance and refines its benefit interpretation logic — so estimates improve continuously over time.
